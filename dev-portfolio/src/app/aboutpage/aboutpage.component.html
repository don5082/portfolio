About Me
Your Digital Footprint, Revealed — and Secured.

In today’s hyper-connected world, personal information is more exposed than ever. Harbinger is a powerful service built to help individuals understand what the internet really knows about them. When you sign up, Harbinger scans public data sources, social platforms, breached databases, and shadowy corners of the web to uncover everything that can be found about you online.

But we don’t stop there.

We compile our findings into a clear, detailed PDF report — built to empower, not alarm. You’ll gain insight into:

- What personal details are easily accessible to malicious attackers

- The extent of your online presence

- Where your information is being shared or sold

- Whether you've appeared in known data breaches

- Potential privacy and security risks

Why Harbinger?

Because knowledge is the first step to control. Harbinger equips you with the awareness you need to take back your digital privacy and make informed decisions about your online presence.

This isn’t just a scan — it’s a wake-up call, wrapped in clarity.
